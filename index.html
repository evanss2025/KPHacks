What is KPHacks

KPHacks is a hackathon for high schoolers to learn and apply coding skills!

KPHacks will take place at King Philip Regional High School in room 320 from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM on May 29th.



A hackathon is an event where you and a team create a project during the hackathon that you're passionate about!

This event isn't just about winning prizes (though that's definitely a perk!). It's a chance for everyone to learn and grow their coding skills while having a blast. Plus, you'll meet like-minded peers and mentors who can help you along the way.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Anyone who is a high schooler can attend!

Do I need a team?

You can either work alone or with a team! Teams are max three people.

Should I bring a lunch?

We will provide pizza and snacks!

What if I don't know how to code?

We will have short workshops that help beginners learn to code and there will be helpers as well that can help with any questions you have.
